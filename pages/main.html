Natural Environment

Jiuzhaigou is a nature reserve and national park located in the north of Sichuan Province in southwestern China. Jiuzhaigou was inscribed by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site in 1992 and a World Biosphere Reserve in 1997. In August 2017, a magnitude 7.0 earthquake struck Jiuzhaigou County, causing significant structural damage.

Disaster Risk Reduction

Mount Agung, a volcano on the island of Bali in Indonesia, erupted five times in late November 2017, causing thousands to evacuate, disrupting air travel, and causing environmental damage. The eruption plume rose 1.5–4 km above the summit crater. More than 100,000 people in a 10-kilometer radius of the volcano were ordered to evacuate.

About This Material

Over the past 20 years, disasters caused 2.9 trillion USD worldwide direct economic losses. It is predicted that in the coming years, people affected by disasters will be increasing because both the frequency and intensity of natural hazards will rise. In today's world, science and technology in economic and social development are the decisive forces. People are getting used to being supported by technologies, especially computer-related ones, in various situations and activities of the daily social routine. It is necessary to use maps and geographic information to understand disaster risk reduction (DRR) in the information society. Based on Web geographic information system (GIS) technology, we construct material for learning GIS and DRR content.


Content

✓ This material contains satellite images, terrain maps, road maps, and other digital maps to understand hazard information.

✓ Train geospatial thinking skills and consider DRR methods by overlaying and analyzing layers of geographic data (such as past earthquakes, floods, volcanic eruptions, and other related disaster data).

✓ This material presents geographical events at various spatial scales, from micro to macro.
