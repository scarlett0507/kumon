Concept of Maps

A map is used symbolic representation characteristics of a place, usually drawn on a flat surface. Maps present information about the world in a simple, visual way.

Vacationers use road maps to plot routes for their trips. Meteorologists—scientists who study weather—use weather maps to prepare forecasts. City planners decide where to put hospitals and parks with the help of maps that show land features and how the land is currently being used.

World Map

A world map is a map of most or all of the surface of Earth. Maps of the world generally focus either on political features or on physical features. Political maps emphasize territorial boundaries and human settlement. Physical maps show geographic features such as mountains, soil type, or land use.
